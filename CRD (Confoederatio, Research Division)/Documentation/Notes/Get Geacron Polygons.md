Geacron PNG (raster tiles) have the following format: `http://geacron.b-cdn.net/tiles/area_2018_Z5_1_8.png?v=20`, or `http://geacron.b-cdn.net/tiles/area_<year>_Z5_<y>_<x>.png?v=20`, with empty images or not found pages being blank PNGs

Additionally, polities are accessible ia the global variable `window.miopais`.
