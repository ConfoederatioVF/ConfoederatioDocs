The purpose of this page is to provide an overview of research notes throughout the duration of assembling Stadestér 1.1 so that both work and sources can be properly traced back to their origin, and to provide some semblance of docuemntation.

By their nature, research notes are informal, and so not all notes have made it into the end database. Neither are they always written with the expectation that they will be read in the future, and so it is important to recognise that some notes may be out of date.

Informal notes/communications between researchers will often be dumped in the **Transcript of Notes** section beneath individual articles. Some transcripts may link to individual articles, and they may be dated. Please check both sections for a list of notes.
